Wildlife

refers to undomesticated animal species that live in the wild without human interference. It also includes all organisms that grow or live wild in an area without being introduced by humans.

Importance of wildlife

Wildlife helps keep the food chain in place and thereby maintain ecological stability. It also helps maintain the stability of the various natural processes.

Wildlife in India

Some of the major wild animals of India are elephant, tiger, lion, deer, and bear.
